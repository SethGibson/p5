Scades

made with processing.js and dat.gui

    This sketch started from a random exercise, I was trying to make a grid of scrolling dots. My intent was to do some video capture and then process the frames. I added a static image for testing, and I noticed that certain images, specifically those with lots of black negative space, gave really good feedback, so this sketch became a simple drawing app to try and capture the interesting motion effect from the combination of color and negative.

    I think I was also trying to learn the ins-and-outs of working with offscreen buffers in processing. Can't get enough of those things now...
