Bubble Chamber

made with processing.js and dat.gui

    Truth be told, the only relation to bubble chambers in this sketch is that some of the results look vaguely like some old images from bubble chambers I saw in a high school science text.  This is an exercise in "thinking inside the box", if you will, as the original version was much more interactive, but I became intrigued by the idea of how much I could condense the ruleset and still produce interesting results. I'm not sure why, but I find the lack of interactivity in this version strangely compelling.  Others may have different opinons...
