Points On A Sphere

made with processing.js and dat.gui

    This sketch was developed for two reasons, one, I was trying to figure out how to distribute points around a sphere for a 3D UI prototype I'm building at work and two, I wanted to learn how 3D in processing 2 works. Nothing fancy here, just shapes and lights, but it served its purpose(s). This one worked so nicely, I even decided to "bring it into the real world" and project it onto a paper globe we had in the lab.

    Accumulation works best at lower resolutions, 4 or 8 are good.
