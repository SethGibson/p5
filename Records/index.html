Records

made with processing.js and dat.gui

    This sketch was the precursor to Bugs!.  I was working through different rotation "algorithms". Not sure why I added the randomization, but at first glance it reminded me of a multi-colored coil of hemp cord, so I started using the term "organic" to refer to randomized radii. No, i'm not a pothead.

    I posted this more for posterity and to remind myself that it's good to let things evolve and go in new directions.  Left click to add points to the sim, right click to clear the sim.
