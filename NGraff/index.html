The Agency

made with processing.js and dat.gui

    An experiment with PGraphics buffers, inspired by Casey Reas's presentation at the 2012 eyeo festival.  My initial idea was to give the agents some sort of structured behavior, but even just a little randomness turned out to be really compelling, so I left it be.  Using a low alpha fade also gave it this "post-apocalyptic graffiti" kind of feel, so I left that in too.

    Click anywhere in the sketch to reset the simulation.
